## Price semantics (client asked, investigated in depth)

- Gamma `outcomePrices` == the MIDPOINT, exactly (bid+ask)/2. Verified.
  That is what the screener shows.
- Polymarket's sports "Games" view buttons show the ASK (buy price); their
  market detail "% chance" is midpoint-style.
- On liquid markets spread ~0.1c so they look identical. On illiquid ones
  (e.g. Bolivian league: bid 10c / ask 68c) mid and ask differ by 30+
  points — that was the client's reported "price mismatch". NOT a bug.
- Our tick collector stores CLOB midpoints, so keeping midpoint in the
  screener keeps screener and history consistent. RECOMMENDED (not built):
  add an illiquidity/spread flag on rows with wide bid-ask. Client has not
  chosen yet between: keep mid + flag / switch to ask.
- Also seen: Polymarket postpones fixtures (slug keeps old date,
  gameStartTime updates) — our kickoff follows gameStartTime, correct.
